01 CONDUCTING RESEARCH
_
P R O B L E M

There is not a dedicated place or close-knit community where musicians can engage with their hobbies, musical skills, and track their progress.

U n d e r s t a n d i n g   O u r   U s e r s

We narrowed our search into three focuses:
1. How they feel about learning from similar hobbyists at their skill level
2. Where/how do they get their motivation to practice
3. Which platforms they are using to share work in progress content

U S E R   S U R V E Y S   &   O B S E R V A T I O N S

We created a user survey and conducted 50 interviews among musicians and other hobbyists in other platforms, such as Reddit. The demographic ranged from ages 13-34.

Key takeaways
- 33 (66%) of respondents would like to share work in progress to receive feedback from their peers
- 37 (74%) of respondents responded positively to learning from similar hobbyists at the same skill level



Key Takeaways

Pain points are significant! And it was important to fully understand our users challenges and what Passionfruit can do to help

  • Target user 1 - Users who often times find it challenging to be motivated to practice their hobby. They always feel that they are not progressing or learning as fast as they should be, compared to others. They wish there was something to keep them accountable, to keep learning and stay motivated.
  • Target user 2 - Users who would love feedback, but posting on popular platforms such as Youtube goes unseen. They enjoy engaging with their hobbies and have joined music discords, however love to post their content on a platform that have people with similar interests and skill levels.

C O M P E T I T I V E   A N A L Y S I S

Youtube

Notable features
∙ Keeps viewers engaged by liking, comments, and sharing
∙ Shows similar videos or recommended videos
∙ Explore page to watch other creators

Cons
∙ No category for smaller users
∙ Music category only features music videos and polished videos
∙ Similar/Recommended only show popular videos or creators with high numbers



Ultimate Guitar

Notable features
∙ Business - an option to go pro with additional features
∙ On explore page, there's a new tab which shows low viewed videos
∙ Contributions (Rank) keep users engaged

Cons
∙ The homepage seems very clustered and overwhelming
∙ Dedicated for complete works
∙ Consistent but no hierarchy - the eye wanders

03 DEFINING USERS
_
K e y   F e a t u r e s

Because this idea will be developed, I couldn't just put some features together. I had to sit down and analyze which features would set Passionfruit apart from our competitors. These key features include:


    Social
    ✔ Explore
    ✔ Share Videos
    ✔ Filtering by Skill Level & Instrument
    ✔ Recommend similar interests
    Personal
    ✔ Progress Logs
    ✔ Individual Snippets
U s e r   S t o r i e s   &   F l o w

I found that planning, creating user stories, and seeing the task flow helped me make better design decisions, as well as identifying tasks that users would perform when using the site.



04 IDEATION
_
W i r e f r a m e s   &   U s e r   T e s t i n g

I created the wireframes using Balsamiq, and conducted in depth user research to find any weak spots. Using scenarios and giving tasks to do, it was evident that "progress logs" were not defined enough.


K e y   T a k e a w a y s

  • The new term "Progress Logs" are confusing to new users and needs clarification in the UI.
  • Most users will utilize the "caption/description" for notes
  • Have a concept that engages users and encourages them to give feedback - Something like "contributions"
